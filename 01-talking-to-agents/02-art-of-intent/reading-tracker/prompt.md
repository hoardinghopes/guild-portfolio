## What I'm Building
A tracker for books I've read

## Who It's For
Just me. I'm the only user. It doesn't need accounts or login.

## What It Should Do
- Let me add a book with title, author, publisher, topic (tags), short review, 5 star rating. Only title and author required.
- Display the list of books in reverse order that they were read.
- Display authors, and list their books
- Search form for when the lists get too long for a single page

## What It Should Look Like
- Clean and simple. Dark mode default.

## What It Should NOT Do
- No social. No recommendations.
- Don't make it complicated. If a feature isn't listed above, I don't want it

## Additional Context
This should work in a web browser. I don't need a native phone app, just a website that works well on a phone screen.
