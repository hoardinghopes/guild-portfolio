## What I'm Building
A simple timer that lets me create named timers (like "Laundry's ready" or "Meeting in 30 min")

## Who It's For
Just me. I'm the only user. It doesn't need accounts or login.

## What It Should Do
- Let me add a new timer with a name (like "Laundry" or "Exercise") and duration (hh:mm:ss)
- Show list of timers that can be edited/deleted

## What It Should Look Like
- Clean dark mode preferred

## What It Should NOT Do
- No reminders or notifications
- Don't make it complicated. If a feature isn't listed above, I don't want it

## Additional Context
This should work in a web browser. I don't need a native phone app, just a website that works well on a phone screen.
